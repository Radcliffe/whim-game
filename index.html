Whim Game

Game Rules:

  • On each turn, a player removes a pile or replaces it with any number of shorter piles
  • Click on a pile to select it, then enter the heights of new piles
  • Enter "0" to completely remove a pile
  • Each new pile must be shorter than the original pile
  • The player who takes the last coin wins
  • Maximum 12 piles allowed
Click "New Game" to start playing!
Whim was invented by Joel David Hamkins.
